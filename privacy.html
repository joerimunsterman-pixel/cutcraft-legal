Privacy Policy

Last updated: 27 May 2026

1. Introduction

CutCraft ("we", "our", "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect when you interact with our content on YouTube, TikTok, or other platforms, and how we use it.

2. Information We Collect

CutCraft does not directly collect personal data from viewers. We do not operate a website that requires registration or stores user information. The following data is collected by the platforms we publish on (YouTube, TikTok):

  • Anonymous viewing analytics (views, likes, watch time)
  • Public engagement (comments, follows) — visible to anyone
  • Aggregated demographic data provided by the platforms

This data is processed by YouTube and TikTok according to their respective privacy policies, not ours.

3. Third-Party Services We Use

CutCraft uses the following third-party APIs to operate:

  • Kick.com API — to access public livestream metadata
  • YouTube Data API — to upload videos to our channel
  • TikTok Content Posting API — to upload videos to our account
  • Anthropic Claude API — to generate video metadata and analyze content
  • Telegram Bot API — for internal operational notifications

We do not share any personal data with these services beyond what is technically required to upload video content.

4. Cookies and Tracking

CutCraft does not use cookies, tracking pixels, or similar technologies. Cookies set by YouTube or TikTok when viewing our content fall under their respective privacy policies.

5. Children's Privacy

Our content is not directed at children under 13. Some content may not be suitable for younger audiences and is marked accordingly on the platforms where it is published.

6. Your Rights (GDPR / EU Residents)

If you are located in the European Union, you have the following rights regarding personal data:

  • Right to access — request information about data we hold about you
  • Right to rectification — request correction of inaccurate data
  • Right to erasure — request deletion of your data
  • Right to object — object to processing of your data

To exercise these rights, contact us using the details below. Note: CutCraft does not directly collect personal data, but if you believe your data is being processed by us, we will investigate and respond within 30 days.

7. Streamer Content Requests

If you are a streamer whose content has been featured by CutCraft and you wish to:

  • Have specific clips removed
  • Opt out of future content sourcing from your channel
  • Request changes to attribution

Please contact us at the email below. We will respond within 7 business days.

8. Data Retention

CutCraft retains operational logs (video metadata, upload history) for up to 90 days for service quality purposes. After that period, logs are automatically deleted.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.

10. Contact

For privacy-related questions, data requests, or content takedown requests, contact us at: cutcraft.contact@gmail.com
